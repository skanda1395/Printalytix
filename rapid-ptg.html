Printalytix | Rapid Prototyping

Technologies

FDM/FFF

Filament extrusion-based technology, best suited for basic fit-and-form testing prototypes

Materials:

  • PLA, TPU, ABS, PC, PETG
  • Ultem, HIPS, PEEK, Nylon, ASA
  • Composites with PLA base and others

Max machine volumes up to 500*500*500 in mm

SLA

Liquid resin-based technology, best suited for fit-form and aesthetics testing prototypes

Materials:

  • Standard Resins:
    Black, Grey, Clear, White & Others
  • Engineering Resins:
    Durable Resin (PP-like), Heat Resistant Resin,Tough resin (ABS-like), Elastic, Rigid (Ceramic-filled), Dental resins and others, Flexible Resin (Rubber-like)
  • Standard Resins:
    Black, Grey, Clear, White & Others

Max machine volumes up to 600*600*450 in mm

SLS/MJF

Powder material-based technologies, best suited for fit-form, fully functional testing prototypes

Materials:

  • MJF: PA11, PA12 and others
  • SLS: PA11, PA12, TPU, PEEK, Polypropylene and others

Maximum machine volumes

  • SLS: 340*340*620 in mm
  • MJF: 332*190*248 in mm

DMLS/SLM

Powder metal-based technology, capable of printing complex parts and very much comparable to 5-axis machined parts involving tough geometries and special materials

Materials:

  • Aluminium alloy, Maraging steel, Copper & others,
  • Cobalt Chrome, Stainless Steel, Inconel, Titanium

Max machine volumes up to 250*250*300 in mm

DMLS/SLM

A short name for Photopolymer Jetting process, PolyJet technology involves jetting of materials of same/ multiple compositions together or in multiple colors which are cured and hardened with UV light

This can print complex wide-range of materials together and hence is used in achieving complex shapes, intricate details and delicate features

Materials:

  • VeroUltra, Rigur, Tango and others, VeroClear, Simulated Polypropylene, VeroFlex
  • Cobalt Chrome, Stainless Steel, Inconel, Titanium

Max machine volumes up to 294*192*148.6 in mm
